# undconsorten

**Inspiring people to grow and build better organizations.**

undconsorten is a McKinsey alumni-founded top management consultancy with 50+ consultants across Germany, Austria, and Switzerland. We specialize in organizational development, team effectiveness, and leadership — grounded in the belief that joy at work and business success go hand in hand. We work at eye level (*Augenhöhe*), from the boardroom to the operational base, and co-create sustainable solutions together with our clients.

## Our AI Advisory Offerings

Beyond our classic consulting portfolio and building tools, we advise clients on how AI reshapes organizations from strategy to execution. Our three core AI initiative areas reflect where we see the most transformative potential:

- **AI in HR & HR Transformation**: How AI changes talent acquisition, people development, and the HR function itself. We help HR leaders understand what to automate, what to augment, and how to redesign the HR operating model for an AI-native future.
- **Operating Model Implications of AI**: AI doesn't just change processes; it rewires how organizations are structured, how decisions are made, and where value is created. We support clients in assessing and redesigning their operating models in light of AI-driven disruption.
- **AI Transformation**: End-to-end strategic guidance for organizations embarking on a broader AI journey — from ambition setting and use case prioritization to capability building and governance. We bring both the strategic altitude and the implementation rigor to make transformation stick.
